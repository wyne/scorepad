ScorePad with Rounds
by Justin Wyne

Easy score keeping for all platforms.

Available on iOS, Android, and web.

No Number Typing

Just tap the top of a card to add points, and the bottom to subtract points.

Multiple Point Values

Tap for one value, hold for another.

Score History

Pull up to see the score history per round.

Multiple Games

Keep all your games together.

Share

After the game, share the final scores with others as an image.
