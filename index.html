ScorePad with Rounds

Easy and beautiful score keeping for all platforms. Available on iOS, Android, and web.

Screenshots

Description

ScorePad with Rounds lets you easily keep score for your games on a beautiful and large interface. It also supports round so you can see the point history throughout a game.

How to use:

  • Tap the top half of a player's card to add a point
  • Tap the bottom half of a player's card to subtract a point
  • To add or subtract faster, try tapping with alternating fingers
  • Tap the arrows to advance through arounds
  • Scroll through the rounds table to view score history
