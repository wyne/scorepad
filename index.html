ScorePad with Rounds by Justin Wyne

Easy score keeping for all platforms.

Free and open source — iOS, Android, and web.

Scoring

No Number Typing

Tap the top of a player's card to add points, the bottom to subtract. No keyboard required.

Controls

Multiple Point Values

Tap for one value, hold for another. Customize both to match your game.

History

Score History

Pull up to see a full breakdown of each player's score per round.

Organization

Multiple Games

Keep all your ongoing games in one place and switch between them instantly.

Sharing

Share Results

After the game, share the final scores with everyone as an image.
